How scoring works

Two factors: how short your chain is, and how rare the bridge players are.
Steps
Number of links from Start to End. Minimum is 2 (start → bridge → end).
Efficiency
1000 ÷ steps^1.4
Fewer hops = higher score. 2 steps gives ~378, 3 gives ~214, 4 gives ~144.
Rarity
20 − distinct seasons played
Each bridge player adds points based on career length. A 5-year journeyman gives 15; a 20-year vet gives 0–1.
Final score
efficiency + (rarity × 25)
Rarity is heavily weighted — a deep-bench connector usually beats a famous one.
