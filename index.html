HOW SCORING WORKS

STEPS
Number of links between START and END. A direct connection (start → bridge → end) is 2 steps.
EFFICIENCY
1000 ÷ steps^1.4
Fewer hops = higher score. 2 steps gives ~378, 3 steps gives ~214, 4 steps gives ~144. Longer chains lose points fast.
RARITY
20 − distinct seasons played
Each player in your chain (except endpoints) adds rarity points based on how short their NBA career was. A 5-year journeyman gives 15 points; a 20-year vet gives 0–1.
FINAL SCORE
efficiency + (rarity × 25)
The shortest possible chain with the most obscure bridge players wins. Rarity often matters more than length.
