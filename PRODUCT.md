# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NBA fans and casual basketball enthusiasts, from die-hard historians to people who just watched last night's game. They play in short bursts: on a phone during lunch, on a laptop between tasks. The mood is recreational. They are not at work. They want a satisfying challenge without friction.

## Product Purpose

NBA Lineage is a daily puzzle game where you connect two NBA players through shared teammates. One fresh puzzle per day (daily mode), plus practice, archive, and live 1v1 race modes. Success means solving the chain in as few steps as possible and sharing the result with friends.

## Brand Personality

Chill, fun, casual. The tone is a knowledgeable friend who loves basketball and makes it easy to play along. No hype, no scoreboard graphics, no corporate energy. Friendly competition, light trash talk between friends.

## Anti-references

- ESPN / sports broadcast aesthetics: heavy branding, gradient banners, cluttered layouts, ticker bars, sponsor slots.
- Generic SaaS / startup: light mode, blue primary, rounded hero-metric cards, "Start your free trial" copy.

## Design Principles

1. **The game is the UI.** Every surface exists to serve the puzzle. Navigation, scoring, and social features are supporting cast, not stars.
2. **Casual confidence.** The interface should feel assured without being intimidating. No empty state anxiety, no punishing feedback. Losing is still fun.
3. **Friction where it matters, nowhere else.** The puzzle itself is the deliberate challenge. Everything else — finding players, sharing results, rematching — should be instant.
4. **No sports-bar noise.** Reject broadcast aesthetics. This is a quiet, focused product, not a highlights reel.
5. **Readable at a glance.** Player names, chain connections, scores: all must land in under a second on mobile, no squinting required.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Dark theme with sufficient contrast ratios on all text and interactive elements. Keyboard-navigable core gameplay. Touch targets sized appropriately for mobile use (primary audience).
